Orange Juice Recipe

Freshly Squeezed Orange Juice

Orange juice is a widely enjoyed drink that is highly nutritious and its recipe is quite easy to make. No additives required.

Ingredients

  • Oranges:a number of your liking
  • Blender

Steps

  1. Peel you oranges
  2. Slice them to pieces for easy blending
  3. Blend them for 2 mins
  4. Serve in a glass
