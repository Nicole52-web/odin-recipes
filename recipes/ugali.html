Ugali Recipe

Kenyan Ugali

Ugali is one of the most frequently eaten food in Kenya. It is a staple food for the many tribe in the country. It is a very easy meal to make and requires less time to cook.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Maize flour
  • 4 Boiling water

Steps

  1. Boil water until it bubbles
  2. Slowly add fluor as you stir
  3. Once the mixture is sticky enough leave for a few minutes before stirring again
  4. Repeat the above steps for at least 20 mins
  5. Serve the ugali with stew of choice
