Fish Fillet Recipe

Fried Fish Fillet

Fish is widely consumed in Kenya. Many prefer to eat fried accompanied by Ugali or Rice.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • 0.5 cup of flour
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Bell Pepper
  • Ginger
  • 0.5 tsp of salt
  • 2 Fishes
  • 0.75 tsp of black pepper
  • 0.75 tsp of paprika
  • Lemon and tartar sauce

Steps

  1. In a shallow bowl or deep plate, combine the flour, paprika, salt, and pepper and mix well.
  2. Dip 1 or 2 fish fillets into the flour mixture. Make sure they are completely coated on all sides and gently shake off any excess. Repeat the process with the remaining fillets.
  3. Pour oil into a skillet and heat over medium-high heat to 375 F.
  4. Working in batches, fry the fish in the hot oil for about 6 minutes on each side, or until golden brown, crispy, and cooked through. Remove and set on a cooling rack to let drain.
  5. Serve hot with lemon wedges, tartar sauce, or homemade sauce, if desired. Enjoy.
